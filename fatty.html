Fattys Cafe

Background

Fattys is a Cafe and Restaurant located in Newington Green. It is a social space where guests are welcome to come in for a coffee, pastry, or brunch from the day time menu. The cafe turns into a restaurant space after 5pm.

Fattys is designed to be a space to work from, gather and make new connections. The spaces hosts various events such as live music, games nights, and quizzes to name a few.
